# Hotel Booking Analysis

The aim of the analysis was to gain actionable insights into customer behavior and booking patterns to improve the company’s efficiency, optimize revenue, enhance customer satisfaction, and develop targeted sales and marketing strategies for a company that is into hotel booking.

I explored the Hotel Booking data and these are some of the key insights I uncovered:

- ✅ The room type that generated the highest revenue for the company was Room 2
- ✅ The top 3 countries with the highest number of destination bookings were France, Maldives and Mexico.
- ✅ The lowest profit generated by the company was in 2014 (140k) and the highest was in 2019 (328k).
- ✅ The customers were highly satisfied.  They rated the hotels on an average of 4 out of 5 stars.
- ✅ The top three countries where the customers came from were Thailand, Singapore and Indonesia.
